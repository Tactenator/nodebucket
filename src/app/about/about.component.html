Who We Are

We are Nodebucket, a group of developers with a passion for creating amazing technology that our customers love. Our Mission: empower our customers to achieve their goals and to keep growing. At Nodebucket, we understand that modern day life can be hectic, which is why we want to provide a top notch task manager that works for you.

Our Story

Nodebucket began as a small task manager app, which was first featured in the Google Playstore. While Nodebucket was useful, it wasn't at first successful. We were but a drop in an ocean of other task management apps. Upon much research and investigation, we created a product that really set us apart from the competition. Nodebucket is the product of much labor and love that keeps our customers happy.
